About Peermarked


Peermarked is a service where one can submit essays, writings and other works to be given feed back by other website users. You can make an essay you submit part of a classroom with your tecahers and classmates, or you may submit it to the gerneral public.You can allow people to edit it or just allow people to give you feedback.

Peemarked in a service made for and by students. It is intended to be a way to recive critisism on a work, from people with many different points of views. Peermarked gives an oppurtunity for work to be looked upon with a fresh set of eyes, helping one get the most out of their writing.

After setting up an account on Peermarked you then can upload pieces to be critiqued by a specific classroom, user, or the general public. One may aslo submit it to a specific subject or field of study, to be critiqued by others that are currently involved or have vast knowledge in that field. An essay my also be submitted to simply be checked for plagiarism or to be proofread. The site prevents anything from being plagarized by not allowing the copy and paste function to work.
